About Clear Bra

Clear Bra is a high-impact film that is placed over your vehicle's paint to stop scratches, Rock chips, curb rash, bug damage, and almost everything that will ruin your vehicle's paint. When installed correctly, Clear Bra is virtually undetectable and also makes cleaning extremely easy.
